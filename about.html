It began on a dark and stormy night.

The way all good stories should- the smell of fresh rain and thunder in the background. Reminding us of just how small we are, like only Nature can. Just like the thunder rolling through the clouds, my journey into Technology has been a humbling but rewarding one. I stand in awe at what I get the privilege to call a 'job'- even if I'm still in the process of getting there.

My Passions:

  • Coffee

  • Technology

  • More Coffee!

Professionally, I'm an avid coder, web developer, UX Designer, and communicator. Personally, I'm a jokester, avid appreciator of the outdoors, and musician. My love for tech began when I pieced together a portable, digital soundsystem- and a few years later, with a lot of support from my family, I decided to chase my passions.

My first step down the road to Technology came through the UX Design course in Google's Coursera program. I was instantly hooked. I realized my biggest passion wasn't just technology- I love creating things that Inspire People- even in the everyday. Whether that meant a great experience visiting a website, or developing apps that help make lives better. After dipping my toes in UX and some deliberation, I began my Associate's Degree in Computer Programming & App Development through MSU Billings.

I'm set to finish my degree in Spring of 2024, and have thoroughly enjoyed everything I've learned so far. I've developed skills in HTML, CSS, Python, SQL, and dabbled in Javascript. Check out my Projects page if you'd like to see some of my work:) My final chapter has yet to come. To be honest- I'm not sure it ever will. One of my favorite things about what I get to do is the endless learning it includes. My focus for now is finishing my Degree, and in the mean time- creating things that Inspire People.

My mission is.

To Inspire People.
